The best labour law consultancy and Litigation firm

Sudhir Sharma and Associates are Labour Law Consultants and Advocates specialized in work pertaining to the Labour Laws and its allied acts for more than 30 years. We provide wide range of services pertaining to Labour Law such as Licensing, Maintenance of Records, Liaisoning, Consultation to the corporate world. We provide complete compliance for - Factories Act, Shop and Establishment Act, Employees Provident Fund Act, ESI Act and its Litigation, Registration and Compliance of Factories Act, Contract Labour Registrations & Licenses, Minimum Wages, Workmen Compensation Act and its litigation, Bonus, Gratuity Act and various other Labour Laws. We provide customized Payroll Management and related services.

We focus on to provide Personalized Attention, Quick and Quality Services to every client on Pan India Basis at value price. We have Experience of more than 30 years & Goodwill among Government Corridors makes us very professional and unique for most of Statutory Compliance related requirements. We have team of professionals who gave complete solutions for day to day compliance related issues.

Being in the business of consultancy since ages, we are in a position to give expert advice on labour laws queries raised by any Establishment, Commercial, Corporate and Industrial clients, as to make such establishment aware of various labour laws.
